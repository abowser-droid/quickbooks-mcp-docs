Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the QuickBooks MCP Server application ("Application") handles your data. This Application is designed for personal use to connect your QuickBooks Online account with Claude Desktop.

Data Collection

The Application accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:

  • Customer information (names, contact details, balances)
  • Invoice data (amounts, dates, payment status)
  • Account balances and chart of accounts
  • Financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, A/R Aging)
  • Transaction records (purchases, payments, bills)

Data Storage

The Application stores only OAuth authentication tokens locally on your computer to maintain your connection to QuickBooks Online. No QuickBooks financial data is permanently stored by the Application.

Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with any third parties. All data remains between:

  • QuickBooks Online (Intuit's servers)
  • Your local computer running the Application
  • Claude Desktop (local processing)

Data Security

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your computer. The Application uses OAuth 2.0, the industry-standard protocol for authorization, and communicates with QuickBooks Online over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your Rights

You may revoke the Application's access to your QuickBooks Online data at any time by visiting your QuickBooks Online account settings and removing the Application from "Connected apps."

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the application administrator.
